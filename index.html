Impacting the Community

A nonprofit organization based in Tanzania committed to improving the welfare of the communities around Manyara region. We aim at achieving sustainable results from development activities and initiatives to our communities, especially with children, women and youth through education and capacity building programs.

Our Vision

Envision a society with prosperous livelihood for women, children and youth.

Our Mission

To enhance the well-being of youth, women, and young mothers through empowerment, capacity building, psychosocial restoration, and the integration of inclusive development.

Our Goal

Our goal is to have IMPACT IN LIVES by providing high impact oriented, cost effective & culturally competent capacity building programs to people in Manyara region particularly; youth, women, widows, and children of school going age.

Projects

The Tanzania Naioth Eagle Foundation (TANEFO) supports a Women's Empowerment Program that offers women sustainable income opportunities through bead and buskets production. The program uses locally sourced materials to create a variety of handcrafted products, such as necklaces, bracelets, earrings and buskets, which reflect Tanzania's cultural heritage and modern fashion trends. This initiative provides financial independence, builds community, and empowers women with valuable skills. By purchasing these beads and buskets, customers directly support women's empowerment and community development. For more information, contact TANEFO.

About Us

Tanzania Naioth Eagle Foundation (TANEFO)

Founded on June 9, 2021, and officially registered on January 11, 2023, the Tanzania Naioth Eagle Foundation (TANEFO) was the brainchild of Jeremiah Ayo, a visionary Tanzanian committed to enhancing the lives of his community in the Manyara rural areas. Recognizing the urgent need for sustainable development and community support, Jeremiah embarked on this mission to address local challenges and foster positive change.

Since its inception, TANEFO has made significant strides in improving community welfare. One of its landmark achievements has been the implementation of several key projects aimed at boosting education and healthcare. The foundation has established a scholarship program for underprivileged children, ensuring they have access to quality education and opportunities for a brighter future. Additionally, TANEFO has launched health outreach initiatives that provide essential medical services and health education to remote communities, significantly improving overall health outcomes.

TANEFO has also focused on economic empowerment by supporting local entrepreneurs and farmers through training and microfinance programs. These efforts have enabled many to start or expand small businesses, creating a ripple effect of economic growth and self-sufficiency within the region.

The foundation’s commitment to environmental sustainability is equally commendable. By promoting conservation practices and engaging local communities in environmental stewardship, TANEFO has helped protect natural resources and enhance biodiversity in the Manyara area.

Jeremiah Ayo’s vision and the collective efforts of the TANEFO team have not only addressed immediate needs but also laid the groundwork for long-term, sustainable development. The foundation's success is a testament to the power of community-driven initiatives and the positive impact that dedicated individuals can have on their surroundings.
